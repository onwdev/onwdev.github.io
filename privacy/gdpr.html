Your Privacy

Onw Dev develops mobile games and applications, including offline games, apps requiring login, online apps, and apps with ads. This policy explains how we handle your data and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable regulations.

1. About Onw Dev

Onw Dev develops mobile games and applications for personal use. We are committed to protecting your privacy, especially for EU/UK residents and children under 13.

  • Publisher: Onw Dev
  • Contact: onwdev@gmail.com
  • Website: onwdev.github.io

2. Personal Information We Collect

The information we collect depends on the type of app you use:

2.1 Offline Apps

  • Local Data Only: Offline apps (e.g., some games) store data like game progress or settings locally on your device. We do not collect or access this data.
  • No Personal Information: These apps do not collect names, emails, or any personal details.
  • No Ads: Offline apps do not show ads or use ad services.

2.2 Apps Requiring Login

  • Account Information: For apps with online or social features, we may collect:
    • Email address (for login or account recovery).
    • Username or display name.
    • Password (encrypted for security).
    • Optional profile details (e.g., profile picture, if provided).
  • Usage Data: We may collect app usage patterns (e.g., time spent, features used) to improve the app.

2.3 Online Apps

  • Metadata: We may collect details like message timestamps or recipient IDs to ensure online features work smoothly.
  • Contacts: If you add friends, we may store contact IDs (not phone numbers) to manage your friend list.

2.4 Apps with Ads (Google AdMob)

  • Ad Data: Some apps use Google AdMob to show ads, which may collect:
    • Advertising ID (for personalized ads, if enabled).
    • Device information (e.g., device type, OS version).
    • Usage data (e.g., ad interactions).
  • Non-Personalized Ads: For kids’ apps or if you opt out, we use non-personalized ads that do not track you.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use information to make our apps work well for you:

3.1 Offline Apps

  • Game Features: Local data (e.g., scores) is used to save progress and settings.
  • No Data Sent: We do not see or use this data outside your device.

3.2 Apps Requiring Login

  • Account Management: Email and username help you log in and recover your account.
  • Personalization: Profile details (e.g., username) customize your experience.

3.3 Online Apps

  • Deliver Messages: We use online content and metadata to send messages securely.
  • Manage Contacts: Contact IDs help you connect with friends.

3.4 Apps with Ads

  • Show Ads: Google AdMob uses data to display relevant ads (personalized or non-personalized).
  • No Direct Access: We do not control AdMob’s data; see Google’s Privacy Policy for details.

4. Information Sharing

We are careful about sharing your information:

4.1 Offline Apps

  • No Sharing: We do not collect or share any data from offline apps.

4.2 Apps Requiring Login and Online Apps

  • Service Providers: We may share data (e.g., email or online content) with trusted providers (e.g., cloud servers) to run the app. They follow strict privacy rules.
  • No Marketing: We do not share your data for marketing purposes.

4.3 Apps with Ads

  • Google AdMob: AdMob may collect and share data for ads, as described in Google’s Privacy Policy.

5. Children's Privacy

Many of our apps are kid-friendly, and we take extra care to protect children under 13 under GDPR-K and COPPA:

  • Offline Kids’ Apps: These do not collect any data or show ads, so they are safe for kids.
  • Kids’ Apps with Ads: We use Google AdMob’s “Tag For Child-Directed Treatment” to show only non-personalized, kid-safe ads. No tracking for kids under 13.
  • Login/Online Apps: Kids under 13 cannot use these apps without parental consent. Contact us at onwdev@gmail.com to provide or revoke consent.
  • Parental Control: Parents can delete data by uninstalling offline apps or contacting us for login/online apps.

6. Your GDPR Rights

If you’re in the EU/UK, you have these rights under GDPR:

  • Access: See what data we have about you (e.g., account details).
  • Delete: Remove your data from login/online apps.
  • Object: Stop data processing (e.g., opt out of ads).
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data.
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.

Email onwdev@gmail.com for GDPR requests.

7. How to Exercise Your Rights

  • Delete Data: Uninstall offline apps to remove local data. For login/online apps, contact us to delete your account.
  • Opt Out of Ads: Disable personalized ads in app settings or your device’s ad settings.
  • Contact: Email onwdev@gmail.com with the subject "Privacy GDPR Inquiry" for GDPR requests.
  • Response Time: We will respond within 30 days, as required by GDPR.

8. Third-Party Services

Our offline apps do not use third-party services. For apps with ads, we use Google AdMob, which may collect data as described in Google’s Privacy Policy.

  • Response: We will respond within 30 days, as required by GDPR.
