1. What This Means

For Kids: Our game is like playing with toys at home! It works without internet, and we don’t know anything about you. Everything you do in the game stays on your phone or tablet.

For Parents: Our game is completely offline and designed for children under 13. It does not collect any personal information, does not use ads, and is safe for kids to play. All game data, like scores or settings, stays only on your child’s device.

Maximum Safety: No internet, no personal data, no ads, just fun and safe gameplay!

2. What Information We Collect

2.1 Game Data (Stored Only on Your Device)

  • Game Progress: Scores, levels completed, or game settings.
  • App Preferences: Choices like sound settings or favorite characters.
  • Local Storage: This data stays on your device, like a notebook only you can see!

2.2 No Personal Information

No Personal Information Collected: Our apps are completely offline and do not collect, store, or share any personal information, such as identifiers, internet activity, or commercial information. All game data, such as progress or settings, is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

2.3 No Ads or Tracking

  • Completely Offline: The game works without internet.
  • No Ads: We do not show any advertisements.
  • No Tracking: We do not use any tracking or analytics services.

3. How We Use Information

3.1 Game Data Usage

  • Game Experience: To save your child’s progress and settings.
  • Achievements: To track accomplishments and unlock features.
  • Performance: To ensure the game runs smoothly on the device.

Important: All game data stays on your child’s device, like toys in their room. We can’t see it, and it doesn’t go anywhere!

4. Do We Share Information?

Simple Answer: No!

We don’t share any information about your child because we don’t collect any personal information. All game data stays on your child’s device.

4.1 What We Don’t Share

  • Personal Information: We don’t have any to share.
  • Game Progress: Stays only on the device.
  • Device Information: Not collected or shared.

4.2 No Third Parties

  • No Third Parties: We don’t use any third-party services.
  • Offline First: The game works completely offline.
